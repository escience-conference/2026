eScience'26 will be held at the Villa Doria d’Angri, an architectural masterpiece belonging to the University of Naples “Parthenope” (Via Francesco Petrarca 80, 80128, Naples, Italy), located on the cliff of the Posillipo hill in Naples, one of the best neighborhood of the city enjoying a spectacular view of the bay of Naples, the mount Vesuvio, and the Islands.

Villa Doria d’Angri is approximately 45 minutes from Naples International Airport and 40 minutes away from the Naples railway station.

You are responsible for making your own travel arrangements.
