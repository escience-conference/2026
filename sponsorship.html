Why Sponsor eScience 2026?

eScience brings together a diverse community of researchers, developers, and practitioners working at the forefront of computational and data-intensive science. By sponsoring our conference, you will:

  • Increase visibility among leading researchers and practitioners in computational science
  • Network with key decision-makers from academia, industry, and government organizations
  • Showcase your products and services to a highly targeted audience
  • Support the advancement of computational and data-driven research
  • Recruit top talent from the global eScience community

How to Become a Sponsor

We welcome organizations of all sizes to partner with us. Whether you're a technology company, research institution, government agency, or non-profit organization, we have sponsorship opportunities that can meet your needs and budget.
